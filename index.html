About Me

Hi! I'm Trey Rachall, a Computer Science student in Texas State University's Class of 2027.

As an undergraduate I am currently working on projects that consist of creating software and AI that pertain to my hobbies such as Jiu Jitsu, mixed martial arts, and gaming. This summer, I am participating at Clarkson University in New York as part of the NSF Sensor Design and Implementation Pipline REU, where I am working to develop an application that utilizes sensors to track humans eye movement using the Meta Quest Pro.

This past semester, I developed a project where I trained an AI model to detect and classify hand gestures. The program utilized Roku's official API, enabling users to control their TV using only hand movements.
